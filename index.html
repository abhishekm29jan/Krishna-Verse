Privacy & Data Protection

Your Privacy Matters

KrishnaVerse is designed to provide a peaceful, secure, and trustworthy AI-powered spiritual experience while respecting and protecting your personal information.

KrishnaVerse may collect limited account information such as your name, email address, and profile image when using Firebase Authentication or Google Sign-In.

Some conversations and personalization preferences may also be stored locally on your device to improve continuity and user experience.

AI-Powered Conversations

KrishnaVerse uses AI processing services to generate emotionally supportive and spiritually inspired responses.

Certain conversations may be securely processed through third-party AI APIs strictly for response generation and app functionality. KrishnaVerse does not sell user conversations or personal data.

Reasonable security measures are implemented to help protect user information and maintain a secure application environment.

Third-Party Services

These services may process limited information required for core application functionality.

The app does not replace professional medical, psychological, legal, or therapeutic advice. Users should seek qualified professional support when necessary.

Children's Privacy

KrishnaVerse is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
